before a dirty group is first created, the ancestor group instances are checked to see if this dirty group is a member of a falling group. 

If it is, all of the rest of the members of the falling group are instantiated as dirty groups having a clone take the place of the original part

this is so that the true originals can be restored even if the "originals" move
